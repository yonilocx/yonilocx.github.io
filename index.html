Biography

Yoni Locx (born Yihonal Sahilu Melka) is an Ethiopian rapper, songwriter, record producer, and entrepreneur from Negele Borana, Oromia, Ethiopia. He is known for his independent hip-hop music, which combines contemporary rap with Ethiopian influences. His lyrics often explore themes of ambition, resilience, self-improvement, and personal experiences.

Driven by his curiosity for music production during his teenage years, he taught himself to use digital audio software by installing FL Studio on a computer in his school's Information Technology (IT) classroom, spending break times experimenting with beat-making and recording. This raw determination set the foundation for launching his independent record label, Raw-Verse Records.
