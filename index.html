Biography

Yoni Locx (born Yihonal Sahilu Melka; 27 August 1998) is an Ethiopian rapper, songwriter, record producer, and entrepreneur from Negele Borana, Oromia, Ethiopia. He is known for his independent hip-hop music, which combines contemporary rap with Ethiopian influences. His lyrics often explore themes of ambition, resilience, self-improvement, and personal experiences.

Early Life

Yihonal Sahilu Melka was born on 27 August 1998 in Negele Borana, Oromia, Ethiopia. During his teenage years, he developed a deep passion for hip-hop and music production. While attending school, he frequently performed rap verses for classmates and friends during breaks, gradually earning recognition among his peers for his lyrical ability.

Driven by his curiosity for music production, he taught himself to use digital audio software. He installed FL Studio on a computer in his school's Information Technology (IT) classroom and spent break times experimenting with beat-making and recording ideas. The activity was eventually discovered by the school administration, and he received a formal warning. The incident strengthened his determination to pursue music, and he continued developing his skills outside of school.

Career Timeline

Yoni Locx began his musical journey during his teenage years under the stage name Løcx Wagøne. In 2017, he independently released several songs through online platforms, experimenting with his sound and developing his songwriting and production skills. Much of his early work was later lost after his original YouTube channel, which hosted his earliest recordings, was deleted. As a result, many of his releases from that period are no longer publicly available. One of the few surviving recordings is the SoundCloud track "Music is my destiny... (prod by Locx Wagone)". Another early release, "Savageness," was originally published in early 2019 and remains available on SoundCloud.

In 2019, he adopted the stage name YONI LOCX and released the single "Beast," marking the beginning of a new chapter in his career. The release introduced a more refined artistic identity and established the name under which he continues to perform. In 2021, Yoni Locx released his debut extended play (EP), titled "Little Heart EP". He has since built momentum by releasing several highly impactful singles and projects, including "Yehiwot Fettena & Struggle In Success," "Struggle In Success (Remix)," "Habeshawi," "Orenda," "Selihom," and his hit single "Ewnetawen."

Raw-Verse Records

Yoni Locx is the founder of Raw-Verse Records, an independent record label and recording studio currently under development. The label is intended to support independent artists and serve as a creative hub for music production, recording, and distribution. In addition to releasing his own forward-thinking records, he plans to use Raw-Verse Records to collaborate with, mentor, and develop emerging musicians.

Musical Style

Yoni Locx's music combines contemporary hip-hop, trap, and rap with rich Ethiopian musical influences. He performs in both Amharic and English, allowing him to reach diverse audiences while proudly expressing his cultural background. His musical style seamlessly blends melodic elements with intricate lyrical storytelling, serving as a powerful reflection of both modern hip-hop trends and his native Ethiopian heritage.
